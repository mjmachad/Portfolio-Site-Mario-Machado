My Story

I’m a UX Designer with a background in ergonomics and kinesiology. I’ve devoted my career to helping and putting others first and this devotion has led me to a career in UX design. My role in research has allowed me to identify pain points, customer experience and work on solutions to ensure they are content. Now I’m looking forward to applying these same principles to an employer that values research and a user-centric approach as the success behind their product. My goal is to become a user experience designer at a large gaming company within the east coast of Canada. I would love to work for an employer that values research and design strategies and also has a fun work culture. As an ergonomist turned UX designer I have experienced in understanding how poorly designed products can impact the user both mentally and physically. My previous knowledge in research and new found skills will help be an asset to any team that I join.
